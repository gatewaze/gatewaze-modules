# Gatewaze Trademark Policy

## Overview

The "Gatewaze" name, logo, and associated branding (collectively, the "Gatewaze Trademarks") are trademarks of the Gatewaze project. This document describes the policy for permissible use of the Gatewaze Trademarks.

While the Gatewaze software is available under the Apache License 2.0, which grants broad permissions to use, modify, and distribute the software, the Apache License **does not** grant permission to use the Gatewaze Trademarks except as described in this policy.

## Purpose

This trademark policy exists to:

- Prevent confusion about the source or origin of Gatewaze software
- Protect the reputation of the Gatewaze project and its community
- Ensure that users can distinguish official Gatewaze releases from forks and derivative works

## Permitted Uses

You **may** use the Gatewaze Trademarks in the following ways without prior written permission:

1. **Referring to the project.** You may use the Gatewaze name to accurately refer to the Gatewaze project, its software, and its community. For example: "This application is built on Gatewaze" or "Compatible with Gatewaze."

2. **Unmodified distributions.** You may use the Gatewaze Trademarks when distributing an unmodified copy of the Gatewaze software, provided that you have not altered the software in any way.

3. **Community and educational use.** You may use the Gatewaze name in blog posts, articles, tutorials, presentations, and community discussions that reference the project.

4. **Factual statements.** You may make truthful factual statements about your relationship with the Gatewaze project, such as "Contributor to Gatewaze" or "Based on Gatewaze."

## Restricted Uses

You **may not** use the Gatewaze Trademarks in the following ways without prior written permission:

1. **Marketing forks or derivative products.** You may not use the Gatewaze name, logo, or branding to market, promote, or identify forks or derivative products. If you create a fork or derivative work, you must choose a different name and branding that is clearly distinct from Gatewaze.

2. **Implying official endorsement.** You may not use the Gatewaze Trademarks in any way that suggests official endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation with the Gatewaze project unless such a relationship exists and has been authorized in writing.

3. **Domain names and social media.** You may not register domain names, social media accounts, or other online identifiers that include the Gatewaze name in a way that could cause confusion with the official project.

4. **Modified software.** You may not distribute modified versions of the Gatewaze software using the Gatewaze Trademarks without clearly indicating that the software has been modified. Modified distributions must be clearly distinguished from the official Gatewaze releases.

5. **Merchandise and commercial goods.** You may not use the Gatewaze Trademarks on merchandise, commercial goods, or services without prior written permission.

## Requirements for Forks and Derivative Works

If you fork or create a derivative work of the Gatewaze software:

1. **Choose a different name.** Your fork or derivative product must use a name that is clearly distinct from "Gatewaze." Names that are confusingly similar to Gatewaze are not permitted.

2. **Remove Gatewaze branding.** Replace the Gatewaze logo and branding with your own. Do not use the Gatewaze logo or visual identity in your fork.

3. **Attribution.** You must retain the copyright notices and NOTICE file as required by the Apache License 2.0. You may state that your product is "based on Gatewaze" or "derived from Gatewaze" for factual accuracy.

4. **No confusion.** Your fork must not create confusion about whether it is the official Gatewaze project or is endorsed by the Gatewaze project maintainers.

## Logo Usage Guidelines

When using the Gatewaze logo with permission:

- Do not modify, distort, or alter the logo
- Do not change the logo colors
- Maintain adequate clear space around the logo
- Do not combine the logo with other logos or marks without permission

## Requesting Permission

If your intended use of the Gatewaze Trademarks is not covered by this policy, or if you are unsure whether your use is permitted, please contact the project maintainers by opening an issue in the repository.

## Enforcement

The Gatewaze project reserves the right to review and take action on any use of the Gatewaze Trademarks. Uses that are not in compliance with this policy may be asked to cease, and the project maintainers may take further action if necessary to protect the trademarks.

## Changes to This Policy

This trademark policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be communicated through the project repository. The most current version of this policy is always available in the project's source repository.
